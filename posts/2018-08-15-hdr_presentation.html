HDR in Luma
2018-08-15

In 2017, my coworker Angela Wei and I gave a talk several times on how we implemented our HDR pipeline in Luma. Luma is our WebGL-based rendering engine that we leverage to visualize architectural scenes at CBRE Build (formerly Floored). The talk was presented at the NY WebGL meetup, a tech talk at Columbia, and we even guest lectured for Patrick Cozzi at his graduate computer graphics class.

The talk gives a brief intro on what is HDR and then dives into the details of how we implemented it in our engine.
